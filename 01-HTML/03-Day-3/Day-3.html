Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes

Rise and shine! There is nothing quite like the smell of warm, fluffy pancakes on a slow weekend morning. Made completely from scratch with simple pantry staples, this golden classic is guaranteed to become a beloved family favorite. Top it off with fresh berries, butter, and a generous drizzle of maple syrup!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Combine wet ingredients: Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Cook: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour the batter onto the griddle, using approximately half cup for each pancake.
  4. Flip: Brown on both sides and serve hot.
